ELEGANT AND WELCOMING

Asturias Beach house is one of the beautiful beach resort found in Midwest Cebu. Specifically in Brgy. Owak, Asturias,Cebu.

It was once a rest house then later developed into an events venue for various occasions and activities such as weddings, parties, trainings, and seminars.

The place is very close the the natures best feature. The serenity of its surroundings are very calm, relaxing, quite, private, clean and has a very hospitable staff with great customer service.
